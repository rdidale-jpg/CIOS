# BT Commercial Digital Twin workspace

Flora now exposes an authenticated Digital Twins product area with BT Group as the only enabled Commercial Digital Twin for this slice.

Rob enters through **Digital Twins**, opens **BT Group**, sees trusted Enterprise Model knowledge separately from new source-backed candidate findings, and can run **Search for new information** against the approved BT FY26 reporting source. The search reuses Financial Intelligence Slice 2C and returns to the BT workspace; reports remain detailed views and the Commercial Digital Twin remains the durable asset.

Candidate financial findings remain outside trusted Twin state until verification and canonical acceptance are implemented. The workspace shows verification pending status, trusted Twin unchanged status, source title, source authority, source location and supporting excerpt for each finding.

Current limitations and Unknowns:

- Only BT Group is enabled.
- Only revenue, operating profit and profit before tax are displayed as source-backed candidate metrics.
- Trusted financial Twin state may be empty until canonical acceptance is implemented.
- Live deployed source accessibility remains Unknown until the product action succeeds after deployment.
- No deployment is performed by this implementation change.
