## Dependencies on undocumented or unsafe context

- Banking-specific examples carry lessons but are not a generic Researcher checklist.
- The Commercial Digital Twin Research Agent Guide is useful but not clearly approved in the production Researcher profile.
- IT-001 and Industry Twin Lifecycle Specification supply necessary Industry Twin content but are Review/Draft and excluded from production packs.
- Supplier, contract, procurement timing and valuation expectations are mostly inferred from Banking closure/backlog rather than accepted operating guidance.

## Required update

**Targeted operating-guidance update.** Add a concise accepted Researcher operating appendix or revised Research Agent Guide entry to the Authority Registry, limited to: Industry Twin start/stop, required research domains, source/evidence checklist, supplier/contract/procurement fields, Banking lessons, Unknown/Contradiction handling, handover format and readiness gates.
