# Commercial Science Registry

The Commercial Science Registry is the governed knowledge library for CIOS commercial cognitive science. It records the scientific artefacts used to explain, test, and improve commercial intelligence systems, decision support, market interpretation, customer understanding, organisational behaviour, and revenue operations.

## Library Structure

| Area | Purpose | Registry |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Laws | Stable, repeatedly observed relationships that constrain commercial systems. | [Law Registry](LAW_REGISTRY.md) |
| Principles | Normative guidance for designing, evaluating, and operating commercial intelligence. | [Principle Registry](PRINCIPLE_REGISTRY.md) |
| Models | Formal representations of commercial structures, behaviours, or causal mechanisms. | [Model Registry](MODEL_REGISTRY.md) |
| Heuristics | Practical decision rules used when speed, uncertainty, or incomplete evidence prevents full modelling. | [Heuristic Registry](HEURISTIC_REGISTRY.md) |
| Hypotheses | Testable propositions awaiting sufficient evidence for adoption or rejection. | [Hypothesis Registry](HYPOTHESIS_REGISTRY.md) |
| Experiments | Controlled validation activities that test hypotheses, models, laws, principles, or heuristics. | [Experiment Registry](EXPERIMENT_REGISTRY.md) |
| Evidence | Reviewed observations, analyses, measurements, literature, and operational findings that support or challenge artefacts. | [Evidence Registry](EVIDENCE_REGISTRY.md) |

## Governance

All artefacts in this library are governed by [Science Governance](SCIENCE_GOVERNANCE.md). Entries should be specific, versioned, reviewable, and traceable to engineering standards or operational applications when they influence CIOS design or behaviour.
