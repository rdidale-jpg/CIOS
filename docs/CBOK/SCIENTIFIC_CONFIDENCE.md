## Purpose

This standard defines how CBOK documents express confidence in scientific claims, theories, hypotheses and evidence interpretations.

## Confidence Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| S0 Unassessed | Claim has not been evaluated. |
| S1 Hypothesized | Plausible claim with limited or indirect support. |
| S2 Supported | Evidence supports the claim, but scope or replication is limited. |
| S3 Corroborated | Multiple evidence sources or methods support the claim. |
| S4 Validated | Claim has strong evidence, defined limits and repeatable validation. |
| S5 Established | Claim is durable enough to anchor standards or baseline architecture. |

## Required Evidence Notes

Authors must identify evidence type, source, limitations, population or context, known counterevidence and confidence rationale.
