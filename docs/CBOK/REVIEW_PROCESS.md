## Purpose

This process defines review roles, gates and evidence required before CBOK documents become authoritative.

## Review Roles

- Author: prepares the document and resolves feedback.
- Domain Reviewer: validates subject matter accuracy and terminology.
- Evidence Reviewer: checks evidence quality, limitations and scientific confidence.
- Engineering Reviewer: checks downstream mapping and implementation implications.
- Governance Reviewer: confirms identifier, lifecycle, traceability and index compliance.

## Review Gates

1. Intake review confirms scope and template fit.
2. Content review evaluates clarity, evidence and confidence labels.
3. Traceability review confirms upstream and downstream links.
4. Approval review records disposition, conditions and next review date.

## Review Records

Each document must maintain review history with reviewer, role, date, decision and required follow-up.
