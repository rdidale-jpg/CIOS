## Governance

Anti-patterns are warning heuristics. They shall prevent generic recommendations and support monitor, nurture or reshape guidance where pursuit quality is weak.

## APAT-001 AI Noise Without Executive Sponsorship

| Field | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Identifier | APAT-001 |
| Name | AI Noise Without Executive Sponsorship |
| Description | Visible AI discussion without accountable executive ownership. |
| Warning signs | Conference posts; vague innovation language; no funded owner. |
| Why it matters | Activity may not convert into demand. |
| Recommended response | Seek sponsor evidence before recommending pursuit. |
| When to monitor rather than pursue | Monitor when no budget, owner or pain is visible. |
| Related patterns | PAT-001 through PAT-010 as context requires. |
| Related playbooks | Sector, capability, executive and competitor playbooks relevant to the account. |
