## Governance

Patterns are heuristics, not laws. They shall guide hypothesis generation and must be validated with evidence before Flora recommends pursuit.

## PAT-001 Regulatory Pressure Creates Transformation Demand

| Field | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Identifier | PAT-001 |
| Name | Regulatory Pressure Creates Transformation Demand |
| Description | Regulatory scrutiny, enforcement, price-control pressure or mandated reporting often creates transformation demand. |
| Typical signals | Regulator announcements; compliance deadlines; adverse determinations; board risk language. |
| Likely commercial event | Transformation, remediation or assurance programme. |
| AI reinvention relevance | AI may automate evidence, forecasting, service triage and compliance workflows. |
| Suggested action | Engage risk, operations and technology leaders around measurable compliance and service outcomes. |
| Confidence guidance | Medium when multiple public signals align; low from a single broad regulation. |
| Related playbooks | Utilities, Energy, Finance, CIO, COO |
