## PROP-001 AI Reinvention Assessment

| Field | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Identifier | PROP-001 |
| Name | AI Reinvention Assessment |
| Purpose | Identify where AI can credibly change outcomes. |
| Target capabilities | Customer operations, field operations, contact centre, asset management, finance, data, governance or platform capabilities as relevant. |
| Target sectors | Utilities, Energy, Telecommunications, Media and Sport where signals support fit. |
| Trigger signals | Commercial pressure, executive priority, legacy constraint, public commitment, data investment, service issue or regulatory trigger. |
| Typical buyer personas | CIO, COO, CDO, CFO, transformation director, risk leader and capability owner. |
| Evidence required | Sponsor, pressure, outcome metric, data availability, capacity, timing and competitor context. |
| Commercial pressure fit | Strongest when pressure is explicit, time-bound and linked to measurable outcomes. |
| AI suitability fit | Strongest when repeatable decisions, high-volume work, data access and human oversight exist. |
| Competitive differentiation considerations | Emphasise Commercial DNA fit, governance, value evidence, adoption and sector specificity. |
| Recommended first conversation | Explore the pressure, quantify current friction and agree whether a short assessment or workshop is justified. |
| Related playbooks | Relevant sector, capability, executive and competitor playbooks plus PAT-001 through PAT-010. |
