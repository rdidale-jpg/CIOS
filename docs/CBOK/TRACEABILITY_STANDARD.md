## Purpose

This standard defines how CBOK documents preserve traceability from doctrine and evidence through engineering and operational use.

## Required Traceability Fields

Every controlled CBOK document should identify:

- Upstream constitutional, scientific, research or evidence dependencies.
- Internal claims, findings, assumptions and decisions.
- Downstream engineering, SDK, application, product or operating impacts.
- Confidence levels for scientific and operational use.
- Open questions and known limitations.

## Traceability Matrix

CBOK documents should include a matrix with columns for source identifier, claim or requirement, evidence basis, confidence, downstream mapping and review status.
