# Banking Sales Director Evaluation Standard

Qualitative outcomes only: Useful, Partially useful, Not useful. No pseudo-precise product score.

Criteria per bank: understand quickly; identify three enterprise-specific changes where evidenced; distinguish sector patterns; identify tensions; form credible questions; explain why now; recognise Unknowns; inspect evidence.

Commercial Reviewer outcome: Useful with conditions. The five-bank workflow saves preparation time and creates more specific questions for Lloyds, Barclays and NatWest. HSBC UK and Santander UK are useful with explicit evidence limitations but need richer UK-specific sources before wider trial.

Architecture Reviewer outcome: Approved with conditions. Observation doctrine, durable Enterprise Models, transient briefings, lineage inspection, Unknown preservation, no unsupported equivalence, no second reasoning architecture, no recommendation/scoring and deterministic runtime are preserved. Condition: promote fixture source acquisition and sufficiency rules before production trial.
