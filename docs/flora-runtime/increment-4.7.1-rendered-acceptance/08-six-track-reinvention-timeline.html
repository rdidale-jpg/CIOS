Reinvention timeline

How UK Banking is likely to move from today’s operating model toward AI-native banking What Flora sees

What Flora expects to happen

Flora expects UK Banking to move from channel-led digitisation into benefit-proved automation, then platform and operating-model consolidation, before AI-native operations become credible under continuous control.

Customer and channel modelNow: App-led service with fragmented assisted channels.1–2 years: AI assistance improves service and next-best action.3–5 years: Digital, assisted and specialist advice become one connected model.6–10 years: Proactive AI-led financial wellbeing with human exceptions.
Operations and workforceNow: Manual hand-offs and duplicated service work.1–2 years: Copilots support staff and reduce repetitive work.3–5 years: End-to-end workflows combine automation and human judgement.6–10 years: Routine operations become largely autonomous.
Data and AINow: Fragmented data ownership and inconsistent lineage.1–2 years: Governed data products and controlled AI use cases.3–5 years: Real-time decisioning and reusable enterprise AI services.6–10 years: Adaptive AI operating layer across the bank.
Technology and platformsNow: Legacy cores and duplicated platforms.1–2 years: API enablement, simplification and selective migration.3–5 years: Modular enterprise platforms and common services.6–10 years: Continuously adaptable architecture.
Risk and controlNow: Periodic evidence and manual control testing.1–2 years: AI-assisted monitoring and control evidence.3–5 years: Continuous control assurance embedded in workflows.6–10 years: Autonomous controls with accountable human oversight.
Commercial buying patternsNow: Diagnostics, pilots and fragmented programmes.1–2 years: Targeted transformation with explicit benefits.3–5 years: Platform and operating-model programmes.6–10 years: Managed AI, ecosystem and outcome-based services.

What this means commercially

Programme demand appears first in diagnostics, benefit cases and targeted transformation; 1–2 years favours explicit productivity delivery; 3–5 years shifts to platform and operating-model programmes; 6–10 years favours managed AI, ecosystem and outcome-based services. Supplier roles move from pilots to accountable orchestration. Account planning should focus on bank-specific triggers, buyers and proof points.
