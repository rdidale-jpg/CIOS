**Document ID:** RG-001

RG-001 is detailed method. It links to the canonical behavioural kernel in `../configuration/Researcher-GPT-Instructions.md` and does not replace Accepted ADRs or owning architecture papers.

## Industry Twin Researcher operating route

Mission intake → Industry scope → Source map → Industry research → Enterprise portfolio → Enterprise research → Evidence governance → Observation creation → Enterprise Model population → Knowledge Graph connection → Behaviour assessment → Hypothesis formation and validation → Coverage review → Human validation → Research-ready gate → Architecture handover.

## Minimum Research-ready gate

Research-ready requires documented scope, source map, evidence lineage, atomic Observations, populated Enterprise Model objects, Knowledge Graph relationships, Behaviour assessment, Hypotheses with supporting, contradictory and missing evidence, Coverage Matrix, Research Backlog, Unknowns, contradictions, human validation state and Architecture Handover Report.

## Supplier, contract and procurement checklist

For each material enterprise or programme, capture supplier relationships, live and recent contracts, procurement frameworks, buying route, incumbency, renewal events, constraints, evidence freshness, Unknowns, contradictions and disconfirming evidence.

## Enduring Banking research-method lessons

Banking research established reusable method lessons only: separate evidence from interpretation; record contradictions instead of smoothing them; maintain enterprise-specific twins before cross-enterprise comparison; preserve supplier, contract and procurement context; and convert uncertainty into evidence demand rather than sales certainty.

## Architecture handover requirements

Handover must include mission scope, evidence base, Observation catalogue, Enterprise Model changes, Knowledge Graph relationships, Behaviour assessment, Hypotheses, Coverage Matrix, Research Backlog, readiness decision, conditions, Unknowns, contradictions and explicit non-claims. The handover stops at Research-ready and must not declare Architecture-ready or Implementation-ready.
