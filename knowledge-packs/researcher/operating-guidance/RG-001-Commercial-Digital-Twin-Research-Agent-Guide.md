# RG-001 — Commercial Digital Twin Research Agent Guide

**Document ID:** RG-001  
**Status:** Operational research guide  
**Owner:** CIOS Research Operations  
**Version:** 2.4.0

RG-001 is detailed method and remains the canonical owner of Researcher methodology and research behaviour. Long-running autonomous Research Missions SHALL operate under RG-002 for persistent Workspace state, checkpointing, recovery, Mission Journal, Escalation Register, autonomous-continuation outcome and evidence-exhaustion lifecycle behaviour. RG-001 links to the canonical behavioural kernel in `../configuration/Researcher-GPT-Instructions.md` and does not replace Accepted ADRs or owning architecture papers.

## Participant-aware Industry Twin operating route

Mission intake → Industry scope → focal supplier or participant context → source map → Industry Twin research → buyer Enterprise portfolio → Market Participant portfolio → programme, contract, framework and procurement research → Evidence governance → atomic Observations → Enterprise and Participant Twin population → Knowledge Graph relationships → Behaviour and market-position assessment → Opportunity hypothesis formation → Account-Participant Position Assessment → hypothesis validation → coverage review → human validation → Research-ready gate → architecture handover.

The route is iterative rather than a strict waterfall. Buyer Enterprise research, Market Participant research, contract/procurement research and Opportunity hypothesis formation should inform one another whenever evidence changes the commercial context.

## Integral participant and opportunity research

For each material Enterprise, programme, need or pressure, identify the commercially relevant participants: incumbent suppliers, alternative suppliers, competitors, partners, advisers, systems integrators, technology vendors, specialist challengers and delivery consortia. Supplier research must not be deferred to a closing checklist where it is material to the Industry Twin, buyer Enterprise Twin, Knowledge Graph relationships or opportunity relevance.

## Minimum Research-ready gate

Research-ready requires documented scope, source map, evidence lineage, atomic Observations, populated Enterprise Model and Market Participant Twin material, Knowledge Graph relationships, Behaviour and market-position assessment, Opportunity hypotheses with buyer-side and participant-side evidence, Account-Participant Position Assessments where participant fit is claimed, Coverage Matrix, Research Backlog, Unknowns, contradictions, human validation state and Architecture Handover Report.

## Supplier, contract and procurement checklist

### Supplier, contract, framework and procurement research

For each material enterprise or programme, capture supplier relationships, live and recent contracts, procurement frameworks, framework lots, buying route, incumbency, renewal and expiry events, extension options, constraints, evidence freshness, Unknowns, contradictions and disconfirming evidence. Participant marketing claims are participant-supplied assertions unless independently evidenced.

## Enduring Banking research-method lessons

Banking research established reusable method lessons only: separate evidence from interpretation; record contradictions instead of smoothing them; maintain enterprise-specific twins before cross-enterprise comparison; preserve supplier, contract and procurement context; and convert uncertainty into evidence demand rather than sales certainty.

## Architecture handover requirements

Handover must include mission scope, evidence base, Observation catalogue, Enterprise Model changes, Market Participant Twin material, Knowledge Graph relationships, Behaviour and market-position assessment, Opportunity hypotheses, Account-Participant Position Assessments, Coverage Matrix, Research Backlog, readiness decision, conditions, Unknowns, contradictions and explicit non-claims. The handover stops at Research-ready and must not declare Architecture-ready or Implementation-ready.

## Progressive Twin Development operating method

RG-001 implements the Researcher mission architecture and does not supersede EI-001, EI-002, EI-003, EI-012, IT-001, MPT-001 or the existing Industry Twin Lifecycle guidance. The method corrects the operating model by making the Researcher continue the next highest-value increment inside one mission rather than requiring a separate user conversation for each enterprise, supplier, programme or evidence gap.

### Outside-in / inside-out spiral

1. Load governed and candidate Twin state, source maps, Evidence records, Observations, Enterprise Model material, Market Participant material, prior synthesis, Unknowns and Contradictions.
2. Create a delta plan: reuse prior state, identify stale or candidate material, name required confirmations and prioritise missing relationships.
3. Build the minimum viable industry frame: industry boundary, control bodies, buyer portfolio, participant portfolio, source hierarchy and materiality threshold.
4. Map transformation across Operate, Transform and Reinvent before detailed synthesis is considered complete.
5. Rank enterprises, participants and control bodies by structural significance: mandate, spend, operational dependency, control power, market-making effect, supplier concentration, programme centrality and contradiction risk.
6. Deepen Tier 1 Enterprise Twins to the maturity standard, then deepen associated Market Participant and control-body material.
7. Model programme, funding, policy, supplier, capability, procurement and dependency relationships.
8. Resynthesise the Industry Twin after every wave and update the gap queue.
9. Continue to the next highest-impact incomplete area under RG-002 until objective completion gates pass or strict EVIDENCE EXHAUSTED is demonstrated. Local constraints, inaccessible sources or open escalations must be recorded in the Workspace and SHALL NOT halt broader Industry Twin development while productive work remains elsewhere.

### Registers maintained during every mission

The Researcher must maintain: scope and tiering register; enterprise maturity matrix; participant maturity matrix; transformation programme portfolio; three-horizon map; coverage matrix; Unknown and Contradiction register; evidence demand queue; cross-Twin synthesis; executive insight quality assessment; completion-gate report; and incremental change log from the prior baseline.

### Enterprise depth and maturity gates

Each Tier 1 Enterprise Twin must include purpose and mandate; citizens, customers, users and beneficiaries; finance, expenditure and funding pressure; operating model; leadership and governance; workforce and productivity; technology and legacy; data, identity, trust and information governance; supplier ecosystem, contracts and procurement; performance, risk, resilience and behaviour; transformation programmes across three horizons; dependencies, relationships, Unknowns and Contradictions.

Maturity is scored 0 = not researched, 1 = superficial, 2 = evidence-backed description, 3 = integrated causal and operational understanding, 4 = decision-grade temporal and executive understanding. Tier 1 completion requires no domain below 2; finance, operating model and transformation portfolio at least 3; overall maturity at least 3; explicit evidence lineage; explicit Unknowns and Contradictions; and a coherent three-horizon trajectory.

### Programme, participant and control-body modelling

Every material programme must connect business or policy goal, pressure or failure mechanism, accountable enterprise and leadership, funding context, operating-model change, technology and data dependencies, delivery participants, procurement route, dependencies, risks, contradictions, current state, Operate / Transform / Reinvent horizon and expected enterprise outcome.

Structurally significant Market Participant Twins must cover strategy, financial and growth pressure, capabilities and offerings, industry and account footprint, delivery evidence, incumbency, alliances, subcontracting, procurement routes, strengths, vulnerabilities, delivery risks, likely future moves, Unknowns and Contradictions. Control bodies must be modelled through their evidenced mandate, decision rights, funding or assurance influence, policy mechanisms, governed relationships, risks, Unknowns and Contradictions without creating a new Twin type.

### Autonomous continuation under RG-002

Do not stop because a long report has been written, a record target has been met, every named entity has one observation, the first source list is exhausted, or packaging is possible. Select the next highest-impact incomplete area and continue automatically. For long-running missions, RG-002 owns persistent Workspace state, checkpointing, recovery, Mission Journal, Escalation Register, mission outcomes and the strict evidence-exhaustion test. Use only CONTINUE, COMPLETE or EVIDENCE EXHAUSTED as mission outcomes. A constrained item or escalation must include missing evidence, materiality, sources attempted, maturity consequence and whether a bounded release remains possible, but it SHALL NOT stop unaffected research while productive evidence routes remain.

### Executive insight gate

Do not promote descriptive themes to executive insight. Every major insight must explain what is changing, why, the governing financial, operational or policy pressure, the enterprise mechanism, relevant programmes, accountable and delivery actors, three-horizon effect, industry relevance, executive decision implication and disconfirming evidence that could falsify the conclusion.
