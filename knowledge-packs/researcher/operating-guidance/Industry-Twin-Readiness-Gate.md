# Industry Twin Readiness Gate

**Document ID:** RKI-003  
**Status:** Operational readiness artefact  
**Owner:** CIOS Research Operations  
**Version:** 2.2.0

Use a non-scoring decision: Research-ready, Research-ready with conditions, Not Research-ready, or Research in progress.

## Core gate

Evidence lineage, Unknowns, contradictions, human-validation state, supplier/contract/procurement coverage and Architecture handover must be inspectable before Research-ready is declared.

## Participant-aware commercial opportunity gate

For an Industry Twin involving commercial opportunity discovery, Research-ready requires proportionate evidence that:

- material buyer Enterprise Twins have been identified;
- material participant categories have been considered;
- significant suppliers and incumbents have been researched;
- contracts and procurement routes have been assessed;
- cross-twin relationships have been created;
- participant assertions are distinguished from independent evidence;
- opportunity hypotheses cite buyer-side evidence;
- opportunity hypotheses cite participant-side evidence where participant fit is claimed;
- Unknowns and Contradictions are preserved;
- missing participant coverage is visible in the Research Backlog.

The gate does not require every supplier in the market to be modelled. Coverage must be proportionate to the stated mission and opportunity scope.
