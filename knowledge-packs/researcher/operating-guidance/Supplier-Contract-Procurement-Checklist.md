# Supplier, Contract and Procurement Checklist

**Document ID:** RKI-004  
**Status:** Operational checklist  
**Owner:** CIOS Research Operations  
**Version:** 2.2.0

Use this checklist to research Market Participants, contracts, frameworks and procurement context as integral Industry Twin evidence, not as a post-research supplier appendix.

## Participant identity

Capture legal and trading identity, participant type, market role, sectors, geography, ownership and strategic positioning.

## Capabilities

Capture capabilities, supported outcomes, technologies, delivery models, accreditations, constraints and evidence of delivery.

## Buyer position

Capture government customers, account presence, named programmes, contracts, incumbency, stakeholders, framework access, partner route, apparent strengths and apparent weaknesses.

## Competitive context

Capture competitors, partners, consortium roles, subcontracting relationships, displacement risks, capability overlap, differentiators and vulnerabilities.

## Procurement context

Capture procurement vehicle, framework, lot, contract value, award date, expiry date, extension options, renewal indicators, buying authority and eligibility constraints.

## Governance for every material assertion

Every material assertion must record source, observed date, freshness, confidence, fact/inference/hypothesis classification, participant-supplied status, Unknowns, Contradictions and human-validation state. Participant marketing claims remain participant-supplied assertions unless independently evidenced. Distinguish fact, inference, Hypothesis and Recommendation.
