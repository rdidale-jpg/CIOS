## Dimension questions

- Evidence maturity: Is the evidence sufficiently current, complete and traceable for proportionate use?
- Structural maturity: Are important market actors, buyers, routes and relationships represented?
- Commercial maturity: Can the Twin explain where demand, spend, pressure and opportunity are forming?
- Decision maturity: Can an executive make a proportionate decision from it?
- Operational maturity: Can the Twin be refreshed, challenged and reused?
- Governance maturity: Are confidence, Unknowns, Contradictions and validation states visible?
