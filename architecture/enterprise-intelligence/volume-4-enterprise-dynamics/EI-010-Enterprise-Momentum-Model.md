**Status:** Draft

## Architectural position

Enterprise Intelligence defines what CIOS knows about an enterprise. CIRM defines how CIOS reasons over evidence and signals. Flora is the first runtime implementation that combines both into operational intelligence.

## Purpose

Define how CIOS detects whether an enterprise is accelerating, slowing or stalling. This paper is documentation-only and avoids runtime implementation detail.

## Why momentum matters

Why momentum matters describes the architectural expectations for this part of the Enterprise Intelligence model. It should be evidence-linked, freshness-aware and distinct from CIRM process mechanics. CIRM explains how CIOS reasons; this section defines what CIOS should know about the enterprise.

## Momentum signals

- Repeated announcements
- Increased investment
- Accelerated hiring
- Procurement sequence
- Programme milestones
- Executive urgency
- Regulator pressure
- Worsening performance
- Competitor movement
- Source frequency
- Signal clustering
