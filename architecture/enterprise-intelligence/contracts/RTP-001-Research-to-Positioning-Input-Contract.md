## Purpose

RTP-001 defines the current Review contract for governed handover from Research to Positioning. It preserves evidence boundaries so positioning work can use research outputs without promoting uncertain material into accepted opportunity truth.

## Required handover contents

A Research-to-Positioning handover must include:

- selected opportunity object and scope;
- enterprise and opportunity context;
- source, Evidence and Observation lineage;
- material Unknowns and Contradictions;
- confidence, freshness and caveats;
- unsupported assumptions that must not be used as facts;
- decision envelope for whether positioning work may proceed;
- evidence demands and next learning actions;
- validation note confirming the material remains Review unless separately accepted.

## Handover gate

Positioning work may proceed only when the handover states one of:

- **Proceed with bounded positioning** — sufficient evidence exists for candidate positioning, with explicit caveats;
- **Proceed for learning only** — positioning may generate questions or hypotheses but not pursuit-ready claims;
- **Do not proceed** — evidence is insufficient or contradictory in ways that would make positioning misleading.

## Relationship to OT-001

RTP-001 defines the governed handover from Research to Positioning for OT-001 Opportunity Twins. This is an explicit Review relationship, not an Unknown relationship. The handover can populate Opportunity Twin handover fields, but it does not mark any downstream positioning object as Accepted.

## Relationship to OPI-001

OPI-001 may consume RTP-001 handover material to create candidate positioning objects. OPI-001 must preserve RTP-001 caveats, Unknowns, Contradictions, confidence and evidence demands.
