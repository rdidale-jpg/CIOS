**Status:** Draft  
**Owner:** Rob / CIOS

## Purpose

Volume 5 defines foundational intelligence concepts that sit between evidence acquisition and commercial reasoning. It explains how CIOS turns evidence into reusable intelligence primitives before CIRM reasoning begins.

Volumes 1–4 define what CIOS knows about an enterprise: the Commercial Digital Twin, Enterprise Knowledge Graph, Enterprise Behaviour, commercial intelligence, human intelligence and enterprise dynamics. Volume 5 defines how CIOS converts acquired evidence into durable intelligence objects that can update those models and support later reasoning.

Evidence is proof of what was published or collected. Observation is memory of what changed, what condition exists or what absence matters. Strategic Signals, hypotheses, commercial theses and recommendations should be built from those reusable primitives rather than from raw scrape fragments.
