## Purpose

Enterprise Intelligence defines the living model CIOS builds for each monitored organisation. It describes what CIOS knows about an enterprise and how that knowledge should be organised, linked, refreshed and used for commercial judgement.

Enterprise Intelligence is not a report. It is not a scrape output. It is not a dashboard. It is the durable enterprise knowledge model from which reports, recommendations, account plans and opportunity views can be generated.

## Commercial Digital Twin

Each monitored organisation should eventually become a Commercial Digital Twin: a maintained, evidence-linked representation of the enterprise as a commercial system. The model should capture identity, leadership, economics, operating reality, technology, market context, transformation pressure and opportunity outlook.

A Commercial Digital Twin is living memory. Evidence updates the model; the model supports reasoning; outputs are generated from the model.

## Target enterprise model coverage

Each enterprise model should eventually contain:

- Executive leadership
- Board and governance
- Financial performance
- Enterprise economics
- Operating model
- Technology estate
- Transformation portfolio
- Supplier ecosystem
- Procurement activity
- Competitive position
- Market forces
- Regulatory pressure
- People and workforce signals
- Relationship graph
- Transformation pressure
- Transformation inevitability
- Commercial conviction
- Opportunity outlook

## Relationship to CIRM and Flora

CIRM defines how CIOS reasons. Enterprise Intelligence defines what CIOS knows. Flora operationalises both.

The Founding Papers FP-003 to FP-009 define the intelligence process: observation, evidence assessment, signal formation, hypothesis validation and conviction. The EI series defines the enterprise knowledge model those processes update and interrogate. Flora is the first implementation that combines CIRM reasoning with Enterprise Intelligence knowledge to support executive intelligence, account planning, opportunity prediction and strategic business development.
