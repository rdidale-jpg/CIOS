# ADR-003: Governed UK Banking Theme Taxonomy

Status: Accepted

Decision: Flora Increment 3 uses one owned, reusable UK Banking theme taxonomy for portfolio, bank focus, briefing, question and peer-comparison projections. Ownership sits with the Enterprise Intelligence architecture, and mappings are human-curated deterministic presentation projections over governed Evidence, Observations, Unknowns and tensions.

Themes:
1. Customer and digital engagement
2. Deposits and balance-sheet economics
3. Cost transformation and productivity
4. Technology, cloud and AI
5. Operational resilience and third-party dependency
6. Brand, channel and operating-model simplification
7. Regulation and control
8. Growth, capital and investment priorities

Consequences: absence is preserved. Banks are not forced into every theme. Theme state may be evidenced, partially evidenced, contradictory, unavailable or not material in the current package. Portfolio briefings remain transient and do not mutate canonical Enterprise Models.
