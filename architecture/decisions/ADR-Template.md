# ADR Template

**Purpose:** Provide a standard format for CIOS Architecture Decision Records.  
**Status:** draft  
**Owner:** Rob / CIOS  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-02

## Title

ADR-XXX — Decision title

## Context

Describe the forces, requirements, constraints and architectural background that make a decision necessary.

## Decision

State the decision clearly and concisely.

## Alternatives considered

- Alternative 1: summary and reason not chosen.
- Alternative 2: summary and reason not chosen.

## Consequences

Describe positive, negative and neutral consequences of the decision.

## Implementation notes

Describe how the decision should affect implementation, documentation, testing or future work.

## Review date

Set a date or trigger for revisiting the decision.
