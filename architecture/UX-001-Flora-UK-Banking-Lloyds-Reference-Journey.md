## Mission

Demonstrate one complete reasoning journey from enterprise change to governed commercial action while preserving evidence lineage, uncertainty and enterprise context.

## Reference journey

```text
Home
→ What changed?
→ UK Banking
→ APP Fraud
→ Reimbursement Pressure
→ Lloyds
→ Explain
→ Opportunity
→ Validation Action
```

## Step 1 — Home

**User intent:** "Show me what deserves my attention."

Flora displays:
- Material changes since last visit.
- Open validation actions.
- High-conviction opportunity movement.
- Open enterprise questions.

Success:
- User identifies UK Banking as worthy of investigation.

## Step 2 — UK Banking

Question:
> What is changing?

Flora shows:
- Industry thesis.
- Current pressures.
- Major transformations.
- Leading mechanisms.
- Confidence and freshness.
- Unknowns and contradictions.

Primary action:
**Explore APP Fraud**

## Step 3 — APP Fraud

Question:
> Why is this strategically significant?

Flora shows:
- Mechanism explanation.
- Supporting observations.
- Regulatory pressure.
- Affected participants.
- Evidence lineage.
- Competing hypotheses.

Primary action:
**Show materially affected enterprises**

## Step 4 — Lloyds

Question:
> Why Lloyds?

Flora shows:
- Enterprise overview.
- Relevant operating model.
- Transformation context.
- Supplier landscape.
- Behavioural indicators.
- Evidence supporting relevance.

Persistent side panel:
- Unknowns.
- Contradictions.
- Open questions.
- Recommended next exploration.

## Step 5 — Explain

Question:
> Why do we believe this?

Reasoning chain:

```text
Evidence
→ Observation
→ Signal
→ Hypothesis
→ Thesis
→ Commercial insight
```

The user can inspect every stage.

## Step 6 — Opportunity

Question:
> Is there a commercially valuable opportunity?

Flora keeps these dimensions separate:

- Enterprise Need
- Provider Fit
- Commercial Accessibility
- Commercial Conviction
- Timing
- Main Unknown

No composite score replaces these dimensions.

## Step 7 — Validation

Instead of immediately recommending pursuit, Flora recommends a governed validation action.

Example:

- Validate executive ownership.
- Confirm funding.
- Verify incumbent position.
- Gather missing evidence.

Outcome:
The investigation ends with a justified action and preserved reasoning.

## Acceptance criteria

The journey succeeds when a user can:

1. Follow one continuous reasoning path.
2. Never lose enterprise context.
3. Inspect evidence for every material claim.
4. See Unknowns and Contradictions.
5. Understand why the opportunity exists.
6. Distinguish Need, Fit, Accessibility and Conviction.
7. Create a validation action.
8. Resume later with context preserved.
