## 1. Purpose

Enterprise Reinvention Intelligence explains why the Enterprise Growth Method, CIOS architecture, Flora, specialist Intelligence Companions, Commercial Asset Generation and continuous learning exist together. It is a founding rationale, not a replacement for the detailed responsibilities owned by EGM-001, accepted Enterprise Intelligence papers, accepted ADRs or the [CIOS Reference Architecture](../reference-architecture/CIOS-Reference-Architecture-v1.0.md).

## 2. Enterprise Reinvention Intelligence

Enterprise Reinvention Intelligence is the disciplined capability to recognise, understand and help shape material enterprise reinvention before the opportunity is fully named or formal procurement begins. It connects governed evidence, enterprise design and learning into inspectable strategic commercial judgement.

It preserves the three horizons:

- **Operate** — understand and improve the enterprise as it works today.
- **Transform** — understand and shape material change already underway.
- **Reinvent** — recognise and help shape a more fundamental change in how the enterprise creates value, operates and competes.

The model is **Evidence + Design + Learning**. Evidence makes the enterprise legible; Design frames the possible enterprise response; Learning improves the quality, timing and usefulness of subsequent judgement. Unknowns, contradictions and evidence lineage remain visible throughout.

## 3. Relationship between methodology, architecture and runtime

**EGM-001 owns the strategic methodology.** It defines the Enterprise Growth Method and is not redefined here.

**CIOS owns the Enterprise Intelligence architecture.** The [CIOS Design Doctrine](../reference-architecture/CIOS-Design-Doctrine.md), Reference Architecture, accepted ADRs and owning Enterprise Intelligence papers define the governed architecture, including the durable Enterprise Model in [EI-001](../enterprise-intelligence/volume-1-enterprise-modelling/EI-001-Enterprise-Model-Specification.md) and atomic Observations in [EI-012](../enterprise-intelligence/volume-5-intelligence-foundations/EI-012-Enterprise-Observation-Model.md).

**Flora operationalises the architecture.** It is the Enterprise Intelligence Operating System: a runtime that works over governed intelligence, without becoming the authority for the architecture or strategic methodology.

This distinction is intentional: methodology guides strategic work; architecture governs intelligence; runtime operationalises governed work.

## 4. Specialist Intelligence Companions

Specialist Intelligence Companions perform bounded work over governed Enterprise Reinvention Intelligence. They may support defined tasks, including opportunity, positioning, pattern, industry or commission work, but do not silently redefine the underlying intelligence, methodology or human decision.

Their work remains bounded by the owning papers and contracts, including [OT-001](../specifications/opportunity-twins/OT-001-Opportunity-Twin-Specification.md), [EI-014](../enterprise-intelligence/EI-014-Solution-Positioning-Intelligence-Model.md), [EI-015](../enterprise-intelligence/EI-015-Enterprise-Intelligence-Pattern-Model.md), [IT-001](../specifications/industry-twins/IT-001-Industry-Twin-Specification.md) and [IC-001](../../operations/intelligence-commissions/IC-001-Enterprise-Foundation-Intelligence-Commission.md). Human leaders retain judgement, relationships, ethics and final decisions.

## 5. Commercial Asset Generation

Commercial Assets are generated from governed Enterprise Reinvention Intelligence, not prompts alone. Their value derives from evidence, design, learning, context, provenance and human judgement; a prompt may assist production but is not a substitute for governed intelligence.

Commercial Asset Generation is therefore an output boundary, not an alternative architecture, methodology, runtime or specialist role.
