# FP-011 — Knowledge Exchange Architecture

**Status:** Proposed Foundation  
**Date:** 2026-07-11  
**Owner:** Rob / CIOS  
**Owning ADR:** ADR-016 — Knowledge Packs as the Standard Exchange Mechanism  
**Normative specification:** [Knowledge Pack Specification v1.0](../specifications/knowledge-packs/Knowledge-Pack-Specification-v1.0.md)

## Purpose

FP-011 owns Knowledge Exchange Architecture: the Knowledge Supply Chain by which CIOS creates, receives, validates, stores, renders and learns from Knowledge Packs.

## Exchange stages

1. Author or acquire Knowledge Assets.
2. Package Knowledge Assets with lineage and declared authorship.
3. Validate the Knowledge Pack contract.
4. Store accepted packages in a Knowledge Repository.
5. Render accepted presentation payloads without upgrading interpretation into canonical fact.
6. Promote selected claims only through the owning canonical acceptance process.

## Exchange boundaries

Specialist GPTs may create account-level interpretation and Twin Presentation Models. Flora validates, versions, stores and renders accepted Presentation Models when later implemented. Flora-native AI should preferentially focus on Cross-Twin Intelligence across Industry Twin, Market Participant Twin, Opportunity Twin, Relational Twin and portfolio contexts.
