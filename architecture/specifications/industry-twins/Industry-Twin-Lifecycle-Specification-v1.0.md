## Purpose

This specification owns Industry Twin maintenance, refresh cadence and lifecycle semantics.

## Lifecycle

Industry Twins are maintained through scoped collection, Knowledge Asset review, cadence-based refresh, contradiction review, market participant comparison, opportunity signal review and Cross-Twin Intelligence synthesis. Industry monitoring and automated refresh are out of scope for Phase 1.
