## Principal decisions

Flora is proposed as the hybrid governed Enterprise Intelligence runtime. GPT workers contribute bounded structured candidate interpretation. Flora owns orchestration, provenance, runtime state, validation, policy and presentation. Governed repository assets remain authoritative. Durable learning is written back through governed knowledge processes. Strong Recommendations require inspectable lineage and human approval before external use.
