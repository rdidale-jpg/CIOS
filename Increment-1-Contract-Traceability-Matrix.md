## Cross-Contract Validation Rules

- No contract creates canonical knowledge: all projection/report/state classes use non-canonical persistence classes.
- Evidence and Observations remain distinct: separate fields and lineage node types.
- Generated reasoning and Recommendations are excluded: no fields for model output, recommendation, score, or action execution.
- Unknowns and Contradictions remain first-class: distinct response contracts and fixtures.
- Inaccessible, unavailable, absent, null, unknown, and not applicable are separate semantics.
- Safe unavailable fails closed and records correlation/audit references.
