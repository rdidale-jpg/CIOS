# FDK-001 — Founder Knowledge Register

**Status:** Review
**Authority:** Founder Knowledge Governance
**Production behaviour:** Documentation only

## Purpose

This register governs the preservation, provenance and recovery of Founder Knowledge. Founder Knowledge may inform CIOS and Flora, but it does not override Accepted ADRs or owning architecture papers, does not constitute public-domain evidence and remains excluded from production Researcher, Assurance, Reviewer and architecture-authority profiles unless explicitly commissioned for a governed task.

## Preservation Lifecycle

```text
Discovered
→ Recovering
→ Recovered
→ Governed
→ Integrated
```

This is a preservation lifecycle, not an architecture acceptance or evidence-confidence lifecycle.
