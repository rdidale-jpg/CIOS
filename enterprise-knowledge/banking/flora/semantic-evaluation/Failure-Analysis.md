## Dominant failure modes

The dominant observed weakness is not corpus inadequacy for the gate. It is the known inability of structure-only evaluation to prove content understanding and the residual risk that content-only execution compresses related AI/cloud and risk statements into stronger dependency claims than the passages support.

## Evaluation design defects

No blocking evaluation design defect was found. The presence of semantic coverage tags in Mode A is a design risk because labels can hint at meaning, but the evaluation specification permits non-semantic labels and Mode A was scored as a structural baseline rather than a semantic pass. No dataset modification was made after observing results.
