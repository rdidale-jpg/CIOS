# Enterprise Knowledge

## Purpose

This directory contains governed Enterprise Knowledge produced through CIOS research and reasoning. It is separate from `architecture/`, which defines Enterprise Intelligence doctrine, models, runtime and governance.

`enterprise-knowledge/` contains what Enterprise Intelligence has learned through governed research. It must not contain architecture papers, ADRs, doctrine, runtime specifications or accepted architectural decisions.

## Core distinction

Evidence proves change.
Observations remember change.
Enterprise Models accumulate change.
Reports are views.

Research reports are therefore not automatically accepted truth. They are views over evidence, observations, models, reasoning and interpretation.

## Governance principle

Files in this directory are governed knowledge assets, not automatically accepted facts. Each asset must preserve:

- provenance;
- evidence cut-off;
- status;
- version;
- scope;
- confidence;
- Unknowns;
- Contradictions;
- human-supplied knowledge labels;
- links to owning architecture.

## Supported asset types

This hierarchy is intended to support Industry Twins, Enterprise Twins, Infrastructure Twins, future Ecosystem Twins, Mechanism Catalogues, Differential Matrices, Mechanism Reconciliation Registers, Participant-Type Variant Registers, Compound Mechanism Registers, Mechanism Candidate Registers, Future Industry Models, Future Enterprise Models, Opportunity Twins, Commercial Assets and supporting research registers.

`Ecosystem Twin` is reserved as an anticipated asset type and must not be presented as already validated.

## Provisional asset lifecycle

Draft → Foundation → Candidate → Validated → Accepted → Superseded → Retired

Do not describe a research output as `Accepted` unless an explicit governance decision supports that state.

## Flora behaviour

Flora may discover and reason over registered knowledge assets but must preserve:

- evidence lineage;
- status;
- version;
- confidence;
- Unknowns;
- Contradictions;
- distinction between source evidence and generated interpretation.

Flora must not treat full documents or reports as unqualified facts merely because they are present in this repository.
